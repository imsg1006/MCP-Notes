Cricket is played between two teams of 11 players each on a large oval field with a 22-yard pitch in the center.
The three main formats of cricket are Test matches (5 days), One Day Internationals (50 overs), and T20 (20 overs per side).
A cricket bat is made of willow wood and cannot exceed 38 inches in length or 4.25 inches in width.
The wicket consists of three wooden stumps topped by two bails, standing 28 inches high and 9 inches wide.
LBW (Leg Before Wicket) is one of the most complex dismissal methods, requiring the ball to hit the batsman's leg in line with the stumps.
The Cricket World Cup is held every four years, with Australia and India being the most successful teams with 5 and 2 titles respectively.
A century (100 runs) by a batsman is considered a major milestone, while a double century (200 runs) is exceptional.
The fastest recorded cricket ball was bowled by Shoaib Akhtar at 161.3 km/h (100.23 mph) in 2003.
Cricket originated in England in the 16th century and is now played professionally in over 100 countries worldwide.
The Ashes series between England and Australia is one of cricket's oldest and most prestigious rivalries, dating back to 1882.
The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, founded in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI).
The IPL currently consists of 10 teams representing different cities: Mumbai Indians, Chennai Super Kings, Royal Challengers Bangalore, Kolkata Knight Riders, Delhi Capitals, Punjab Kings, Rajasthan Royals, Sunrisers Hyderabad, Gujarat Titans, and Lucknow Super Giants.
Mumbai Indians are the most successful IPL team with 5 titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020), followed by Chennai Super Kings with 4 titles.
The IPL follows an auction system where teams bid for players, with a salary cap of 100 crores INR per team for the 2024 season.
Virat Kohli holds the record for most runs in IPL history with over 7,000 runs, while Lasith Malinga has the most wickets with 170 dismissals.
The IPL is typically held from March to May each year, with matches played across various venues in India and occasionally overseas.
Chris Gayle holds the record for the highest individual score in IPL with 175 not out, achieved while playing for Royal Challengers Bangalore in 2013.
The IPL has revolutionized cricket economics, becoming one of the world's most valuable sports leagues with a brand value exceeding $10 billion.
MS Dhoni is the most successful IPL captain with 4 titles, all won with Chennai Super Kings, and is known for his strategic brilliance and finishing abilities.
The IPL playoffs format includes Qualifier 1, Eliminator, Qualifier 2, and the Final, with the top 4 teams from the league stage qualifying for playoffs.
